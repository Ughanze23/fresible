About Fresible

Fresible Company limited is a company which focuses on providing state of the art services in four major categories, which are ICT, Entertainment and Events Management, Law Repository and Digital Marketing.

Fresible as brand is focused on making the world a better place with technology and has set itself the goal of becoming the largest tech comapny in the world providing real and timely solutions.

The Company office is at 65, Ogunlana Drive, Surulere, Lagos.

The Company was established in 2013, still growing and looking to expand in other to be able to attend effectively and efficient to its growing list of clients.

The Company was founded by Fred Oyetayo, he holds a degree in law from Afe babalola university and is also a full stack web developer.
